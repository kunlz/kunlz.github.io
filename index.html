Welcome to My Homepage

I am currently working at the Institute of Tibetan Plateau Research, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ITPCAS) as a post doctoral researcher. My research interests mainly focus on terrestrial water cycle, global evapotranspiration and remote sensing. My latest project is try to estimate the total amount of irrigation water use based on the soil moisture and precipitation data at global scale.
